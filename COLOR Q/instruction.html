INSTRUCTIONS

EASY mode:
There are two type of colour buttons at bottom.
You have to tap on the button of colour given in the first tab.
You have to score as much as you canin the given time limit.
Even a single wrong colour will end your game.
The tabs automatically change after two seconds.



MEDIUM mode:
It is same as that of EASY mode but the colour of tabs will not automatically change and instead there wil be three colours.



HARD mode:
The gameplay is still the same instead there are three colours as well as tabs will change after two seconds.
